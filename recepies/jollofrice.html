Jollof Rice: A Celebration Meal.

In West-Africa, the Jollof Rice has been the most highly respected and most prefered meal for celebrations, it conforms to all taste buds.

More details bout this recepie incoming.

Ingredients required to prepare this recepie are as follows:

  • Rice
  • Tomato
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Meat: Chicken or Beef
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps required in preperation for this recepie are as follows:

  1. Parboil the rice (remove the starch).
  2. Boil your meat with your prefered spice (containing at least: Salt and onion).
  3. Fry the meat.
  4. Blend your Tomato, Pepper and Onions together into paste.
  5. Fry the paste, add the meat broth for taste and flavour.
  6. Add the parboiled rice and a little amout of water and then stair properly.
  7. Allow water content to dry and the stair again, add fresh and neatly diced onion.
  8. Enjoy your meal.
